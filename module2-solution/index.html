The Colors

RED

  • Red is the first color babies can see after black and white.
  • It's used to warn people of danger.
  • Kings in the Middle Ages wore red to display their God-given right to the throne.

BLUE

  • Blue is calming.
  • The sky is blue its universal connection.
  • Blue is rarely seen in nature.

GREEN

  • Green signifies nature and life.
  • In China, green jade represents virtue and beauty.
  • It is the color of chlorophyll, which helps in the photosynthesis in plants.
